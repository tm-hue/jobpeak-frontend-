OTR driver pay in 2026

OTR (over-the-road) is the highest-volume CDL category — and the most competitive for carriers to recruit. Pay has increased meaningfully since 2022 as the driver shortage persists. Here is what the market actually looks like for solo and team OTR in 2026.

Team OTR pay

Team OTR averages 5,000–6,000 miles per week split between two drivers. At $0.72–$0.85 CPM split, each driver earns the equivalent of a high solo OTR rate with significantly more miles. Team is the fastest path to $90K+ in CDL earnings.

What affects OTR pay most

  • Miles consistency — the difference between a carrier that delivers 2,800 miles/week consistently vs one that averages 2,200 is $8,000–$12,000 per year at the same CPM.
  • Freight type — flatbed, reefer, and tanker add $0.04–$0.15 CPM above dry van OTR rates.
  • Sign-on bonus structure — OTR sign-on bonuses typically run $3,000–$10,000 in 2026, paid in installments over 6–12 months.
  • Benefits package — health insurance, 401K, and paid time off add $8,000–$15,000 in value annually on top of base CPM.

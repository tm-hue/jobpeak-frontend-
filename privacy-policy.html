2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with job listings or submit inquiries through JobPeak.io, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • General location or commute information
  • Employment preferences and job search criteria
  • Resume or work history information where submitted
  • SMS opt-in consent, including timestamp and source URL

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit jobpeak.io, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time on page, and referring URLs
  • UTM parameters and advertising source attribution data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Lead Ads — when you respond to a job ad, your name, phone number, and email may be transmitted to us as the ads agency of record (AOR)
  • TikTok Lead Generation Ads — same as above
  • Career site partners and job distribution networks
  • Partner application platforms including appfunnels.io subdomains

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with relevant CDL driver job opportunities
  • Route your application or inquiry to the appropriate carrier or employer
  • Contact you via SMS, email, or phone regarding job opportunities and application status
  • Send recruiting communications you have consented to receive

  • Improve and maintain the JobPeak.io platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. SMS Communications

5.1 SMS Opt-In

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box on any form associated with JobPeak.io or a linked application platform, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Digital Marketing Gen, Inc. d/b/a Audience Target. Consent to receive SMS messages is never required as a condition of any purchase or service.

5.2 Types of Messages

Opted-in contacts may receive the following types of SMS messages: job opportunity notifications, application status updates, interview scheduling confirmations, recruiter follow-up, and document reminders.

5.3 Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your application activity and job search status. If you have any questions about your text plan or data plan, it is best to contact your wireless provider.

5.4 Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

5.5 Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us. Reply HELP for assistance or contact us at inquiries@digitalmarketinggen.com or (516) 494-7447.

5.6 Carrier Liability

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

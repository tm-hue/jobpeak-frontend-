FAQ

Questions before you post

Is there a contract or commitment?
No. Month-to-month, always. Cancel anytime from your dashboard — no cancellation fees, no questions asked. Your posting stays live for the remainder of the paid month.
How does billing work exactly?
$197 is charged when you post. It auto-renews every 30 days — that's $6.57/day. You'll see the renewal in your dashboard before it processes. Cancel anytime and your posting stays live until the end of the current period.
How is this different from Indeed?
Indeed charges per click from a general audience — most of whom don't have a CDL. Jobpeak is $6.57/day flat, CDL drivers only, with Census-ranked targeting that puts your listing in front of drivers in the right cities — not just the nearest zip codes.
What if I don't get applications?
Contact us and we'll audit your posting — pay rate, home time language, and requirements are the three most common reasons CDL postings underperform. We'll tell you exactly what to fix.
Can I post multiple jobs?
Yes — each posting is priced separately at $197/month. Volume pricing available for fleets with ongoing hiring needs — Hire CDL Drivers contact us.

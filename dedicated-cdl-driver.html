Why dedicated recruiting is different — and easier

Dedicated is the easiest CDL position to recruit for when described correctly. Drivers who want dedicated are choosing stability over maximum miles. They are not looking for the highest CPM — they are looking for the most predictable life. Your posting needs to speak to that directly.

  • Lead with stability, not just pay — "Same lanes every week" is as powerful as the CPM rate for a dedicated driver candidate.
  • "Single customer" is a selling point — say it. Drivers who have dealt with chaotic dispatch see dedicated as a genuine relief.
  • Be honest about customer interaction — dedicated usually means more customer-facing time than OTR. Some drivers want that, some do not. Say it upfront so you attract the right people.
  • Dedicated home time is more reliable — if your dedicated account genuinely gets drivers home every Friday, that is more valuable than an OTR position that claims "home weekly."
